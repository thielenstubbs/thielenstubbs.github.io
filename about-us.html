About Thielen Stubbs Avocats

Thielen Stubbs Avocats LLC


What matters in today’s business environment are fast turnaround times and cost-effective solutions. Thielen Stubbs Avocats is one of the top law firms, with over 50 practitioners, and many years of experience in high-level, complex commercial work.

The firm’s focus is on “what’s best for the client” and striving for excellence, in both offering and service. Thielen Stubbs Avocats is committed to providing clients with cost-effective outcomes by ensuring work is done at the right level, and in the manner that best suits clients’ preferences and pricing structures.

Strategic Partner

While many recognize the unique opportunities for trade, investment and growth that the world represents, predicting in-country and across regions risks is not easy, particularly given the rate of exponential change impacting multiple indicators, whether economic, political, technological or social. Thielen Stubbs Avocats is an independent law firm with fully integrated offices around the world. The firm offers clients many years’ accumulated know-how and solid experience, whether doing business in-country or cross-continent, helping to minimize risk and leverage business opportunities across and within Europe.

Thielen Stubbs Avocats has lawyers qualified to practice English, French and Spanish, as well as practitioners who are fluent in African and other international languages, including Mandarin, Cantonese, and Portuguese. The firm also has dedicated China, UAE and India practice groups, which focus on the European strategies of these countries.

Thielen Stubbs Avocats also offers a unique possibility to integrate all aspects of structuring clients’ investments in relation to all commercial areas of law, tax, IP and fiduciary.

Customer Service Benefit

The attorneys and staff at Thielen Stubbs Avocats, pride themselves on delivering the highest level of customer service. We respond to your phone calls and emails on the same day. We keep you up-to-date on the progress of your case. We clearly explain the process so you understand what is happening in your case and make informed decisions. We advance case costs to relieve the financial stress associated with a personal injury.

We only charge you lawyer's fees if we recover compensation for you. We have staffs who fluently speaks many languages and can interpret for you if you are more comfortable with your native language.

Our mission is to recover the highest damages possible in your claim and to make the process as smooth and stress-free as we can. We truly care about your case and your personal well-being, a fact that is obvious from our emphasis on customer service.

 

Thielen Stubbs Avocats are an independent, confidential practitioners. If you have a question, do not hesitate to contact someone from one of our offices.
